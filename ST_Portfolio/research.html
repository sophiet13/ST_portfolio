Research Project at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore

About my Project

February - June 2019

As part of my degree, I am requried to compelte a research project and I was 1 of 4 students fortunate enough to be given the opportunity to complete a 15-week research project in Singaproe.

My project focused on wastewater treatment and I was based at the Nanyang Environment And Water Research Institute (NEWRI). Specifically, my project involved creating different catalysts in the lab and testing the effect of these catalysts on dye degradation, under different conditions.

The opportunity required me to adapt to new learning and living environments and enabled me to learn about the and lifestyle in Singapore.

The practical side of the project developed my lab skills, organisation, communication and attention to detail. In comaprison, the written report and analysis of data enhanced my system skills in Excel and Word.
