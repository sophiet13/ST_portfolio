What is HerCapital?

SEO London

HerCapital is a year-long leadership programme which targets female university students. The programme aims to close the confidence gap by exposing associates to professional development workshops, skills training and regular challenges.

Highlights of the Programme

My favorite part of the programme has been the week-long Technology Training offered to myself and my fellow HerCapital Associates.

Prior to the week I had never coded anything myself aside from struggling through MATLAB at university. From this experience, I was terrified for the week ahead. However, our teacher, Steve, from Sparta Global made the week interactive and fun showing me that I my worry was unneccessary.

Over the course of the week, I learnt how to structure a webiste in HTML and I also learnt how to use CSS to change the design, layout and responsiveness of a webpage
